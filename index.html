Origins & Evolution

The Journey
from Jersey

Jonathan Cepeda wasn't built in a gym — he was built through life.

Raised in Jersey City, he grew up in an environment where toughness wasn't optional. Fighting came naturally, but boxing didn't enter his life until the age of twenty. What some would call a late start became his advantage — because the lessons were already there.

After moving to Florida at sixteen, he experienced a completely different world. That contrast gave him the grit of the streets and the awareness to move through any environment.

Today, as a professional fighter with a 19–2 record and a reputation for finishing fights, Cepeda brings more than power into the ring. He brings discipline, control, and mindset.

Because to him, the fight is never just physical — it's mental and spiritual.
